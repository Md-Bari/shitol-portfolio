# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

The primary audience is recruiters and hiring teams evaluating Md Rofiqul Bari for AI engineering, Laravel, and full-stack software development roles. They are scanning quickly for professional credibility, proof of real work, technical range, communication clarity, and a clear path to resume, GitHub, LinkedIn, and contact details.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio exists to present Rofiqul Bari as a professional, recruiter-ready AI engineer and full-stack developer. Success means a recruiter can understand his strengths within the first viewport, trust the quality of his work through project evidence, and move easily toward hiring actions such as viewing the resume, checking GitHub, or making contact.

## Brand Personality

Innovative, adaptive, scalable. The voice should feel technically confident, professional, precise, and forward-looking without becoming childish, gimmicky, or inflated.

## Anti-references

Avoid childish visual treatments, playful novelty for its own sake, generic AI-template aesthetics, overused cyber decorations without substance, and portfolio sections that feel copied from a template rather than shaped around real technical credibility.

## Design Principles

- Recruiter-first clarity: make role, strengths, evidence, and next actions obvious without requiring deep exploration.
- Proof over noise: use projects, technologies, outcomes, and contact routes as the persuasive core rather than decorative spectacle.
- Professional futurism: keep the dark AI/engineering atmosphere, but make it polished, mature, and controlled.
- Motion with purpose: animations should guide attention and imply craft, never slow scanning or feel childish.
- Scalable structure: every section should support future additions to projects, experience, skills, and research without visual clutter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive states. Preserve keyboard navigation, visible focus states, readable text on dark surfaces, reduced-motion alternatives for animation-heavy areas, and clear labels for recruiter-critical actions such as resume, contact, GitHub, and LinkedIn.
